MCS on Behalf of ZuConnect: Celebrating Our Success with SmarKo®Technology in Istanbul.

5. December 2023

Share this:

We are excited to announce MCS’s remarkable achievement at the Zuzalu Hackathon in Istanbul. Our team secured 1st place in the DeSci category and 2nd place in the Zero-Knowledge track. Zuzalu is an unprecedented pop-up city experiment that brings together leading innovators in crypto, AI, governance, decentralized science, and culture for a two-week popup village in the heart of Istanbul.  This event marks a significant milestone for MCS. Our co-founder, Celin Mousa , along with the support of the Zuzalu community,Xian Zheng,and Daniel Knobelsdorf . This success is more than just a triumph for MCS; it stands as a testament to the transformative potential of SmarKo® technology in the industry. This recognition has not only honored our efforts but also opened exciting new opportunities for our company and stakeholders. We are eager to use this momentum to foster further innovation and make a substantial impact in our field.
